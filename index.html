What do I do?

I work as a Vendor Manager in fashion. That means I largely work on three things:

  1. Project management: I manage EU-impacting Amazon Fashion projects. I'm currently working on a project to improve vendor and VM experience. As a PM I work cross-functionally with a number of different teams and use Agile methodology and regularly use Trello and Jira adjacent tools.
  2. Performance management: I work with our vendors to create joint goals and action-plans, fostering accountability on both sides. This often involves working with departments across the organisation, including marketing and advertising and requires excellent stakeholder management with both internal departments and external vendors.
  3. Buying: I make data driven decisions on what selection to buy, analysing what customers have bought before to predict what we should focus on in future.
